The Historic Lucas House



The Bunk+Brew Historic Lucas House is the newest and ONLY "experience" lodging for the adventure traveler in downtown Bend, Oregon.

2.5 blocks from the Bend Ale Trail, and just miles away from some of the most beautiful nature oregon has to offer, the Lucas House, built in 1910 is also Bendâ€™s first and oldest brick building.

Edward Lucas, the house's original owner was a lawyer, and Bend's very first car owner! He lived in the hosue with his wife and three children for 30 years, before selling it after his wife's death. The family that moved it then painted several murals around the home, which are still visible on the walls today. The character of this house remains strong today, as travellers come from all over and call it home for the duration of their stay. The home-y feel helps foster a communal environment that is immediate upon arrival

Come visit us at our beer garden !


Bunk and Brew's beer garden, open since 2019 is a great place to come socialize, or unwind. Located in the back of our hostel, the beer garden features two food trucks, and our own truck Old Iron Wood Taps, where we serve local beers on tap.

The garden also features a small stage where we ocasionally host live music and other events. In the winter we also put up clear igloos to keep out the cold!

The Perks


Staying at bunk and brew has many perks! We are always in the process of growing and expanding the amenities that we offer to our guests. In addition to on-site amenities, we also frequently take our guests on excursions in the area including river floats, brewery visits, hikes, bar crawls, and all other sorts of adventures.

Our location is walking distance from downtown Bend. Our staff knows the area well and are always willing to show people around!
